Educational Programs

Currently Offering
  • Animate My Action Plan

    This 22-lesson curriculum provides hands-on media arts education to prepare Youth ChalleNGe cadets for successful futures in the 21st Century workforce. Cadets use iPads to learn entry-level skills in photography, video production, audio engineering, graphic design, and computer coding.

    Learn more about Animate My Action Plan

    A version for the general high school population is in development. Please inquire.

  • Animate Your Tobacco Awareness Messages Remote Learning Workshop

    Facilitated via Zoom, middle and high school students work in teams to create animated public service announcements. They write awareness scripts with titles and morals, design storyboards to plan the art and animation for five scenes, create all original artwork, and record Voiceover narration in just four hours. Royer Studios completes post-production to deliver broadcast-quality PSAs. Read more.

  • Speak Out Against Tobacco and Vaping - Classroom or Remote Workshop

    In this 3-part experiential workshop, middle and high school students identify factors within their communities that contribute to youth tobacco-use and vaping and collaborate to produce podcasts that propose environmental prevention strategies to empower listeners to create a healthier, tobacco-free environment.

  • TUPE PSA Video Production Workshop - Classroom or Remote Workshop

    This 4-lesson hands-on workshop provides a step-by-step guide for middle and high school students to develop, produce, and distribute live action PSAs focused on tobacco-use and vaping prevention.
